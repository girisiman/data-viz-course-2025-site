General Overview

This module offers a practical introduction to data science, with a strong emphasis on statistical modeling, data visualization, and interpreting real-world data. Building upon the machine learning foundations from module 5CS037, it expands students' understanding through hands-on work with diverse datasets and applied scenarios.

Students will develop interdisciplinary competencies in statistical reasoning, computational programming, and effective communication. By integrating theoretical insights with practical application, the module equips students to tackle complex, data-driven problems and translate analytical findings into actionable insights.

Difficulty Assessment

Level: Intermediate
Recommended Background: Basic knowledge of Python programming, statistics, and introductory machine learning concepts (such as those taught in module 5CS037).

This module is designed to challenge students to apply foundational concepts in new contexts, work with real datasets, and effectively communicate their insights. While it is not entry-level, it is suitable for students who are comfortable with core programming and data analysis techniques.
